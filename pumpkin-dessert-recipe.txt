Ingredients:
Base:
    1 pkg. yellow cake mix (reserve 1 cup for topping)
    1/2 cup butter melted
    1 egg

Filling:
    3 cups or 1 lb. 14 oz. can Libbies pumpkin pie mix
    2 eggs
    2/3 cup milk

Topping:
    1 cup cake mix
    1/4 cup sugar
    1 tsp. cinnamon
    1/4 cup butter

Instructions:
    * Grease bottom only of 13 in. X 9 in. cake pan
    * Mix ingredients for base and press in pan
    * Mix filling ingredients and pour over base
    * Mix topping ingredients and sprinkler over filling
    * Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes

    * Serve cold
